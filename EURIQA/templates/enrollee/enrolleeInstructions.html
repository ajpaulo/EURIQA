Instructions

General Instructions:

  1. This exam is time-limited. Make sure you are prepared before starting the exam.
  2. Ensure you have a stable connection before answering.
  3. Make sure you are in a well-lit and distraction-free area while taking the exam.
  4. For the duration of the exam, make sure your camera is functioning for the facial recognition system.
  5. Position yourself right in front of the camera. Make sure you are recognized by the facial recognition system.
  6. The system will warn or flag you when the following suspicious actions are detected: (1) an unknown face is recognized by the system (2) when the system detects tab changing. Avoid doing the mentioned actions.
  7. After the maximum flags has been reached, you will be forced to restart the exam with the remaining time you have.
  8. Contact your enrollment officer when a problem arises.
