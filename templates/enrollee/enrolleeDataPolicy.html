Data Privacy Policy


PRIVACY NOTICE: By accomplishing this form, you hereby authorize that the following personal information will be collected, including but not limited to the email address, full name, program enrolled, year level, gender, CIT ID Number, and contact number. This personal information will be used solely for the purposes of gaining a better understanding of student motivation through research study and of creating programs out from this data.

The information you provided in this form will be used within CIT University and will not be shared with any outside parties unless there is prior written consent from you. No personal identifying information will be used when discussing or reporting data. The data will be stored for as long as needed for the profiling of the respondents. CIT University respects your rights as a data subject under the Data Privacy Act.

If you have further questions and concerns regarding the processing of your personal information, you are welcome to contact our Data Protection Officer via dpo@cit.edu.
